What is Gamitify? GATO is the first Binance Smart blockchain plugin using NFT to gamify communities and reward content creators of long-form content all over the world. In a world flooded with meaningless, short-lived, instant content, our mission is to balance the situation by encouraging experts to build and monetize from an extensive network of knowledge and ideas.
